1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, enhance user experiences, and provide information to website owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Digital Mythri uses the following types of cookies:

a) Strictly Necessary Cookies

  • These cookies are essential for you to navigate our website and use its features. Without these cookies, certain services or functionalities cannot be provided.

b) Performance Cookies

  • These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website (e.g., pages visited, time spent on each page). We use this data to improve our website’s performance and user experience.

c) Functionality Cookies

  • These cookies remember your preferences and enable us to personalize content for you. They may remember details such as your language preference or region.

d) Targeting or Advertising Cookies

  • These cookies track your browsing habits and help us deliver relevant advertisements and offers based on your interests. They are usually placed by third-party advertising networks.

e) Analytics Cookies

  • These cookies collect aggregated information to help us understand how our website is used, identify trends, and improve our services.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use cookies provided by trusted third parties, such as Google Analytics, to help us analyze website usage and improve our services. Third-party cookies are subject to the privacy policies of the respective providers.

For example:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics is a web analytics service provided by Google that uses cookies to help us understand how users engage with our website. You can learn more about how Google uses data from cookies at Google's Privacy Policy.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Provide essential functionalities of our website
  • Analyze and improve website performance
  • Personalize content and advertisements based on your interests
  • Gain insights into user interactions with our website and services
